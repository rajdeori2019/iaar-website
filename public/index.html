Our mission

A Safe Haven for TA Professionals

I AM A RECRUITER is built for the people behind every great hire. We provide a space where talent acquisition professionals can connect authentically — sharing challenges, celebrating wins, and growing together without the noise of self-promotion.

From Bangalore to Hyderabad to Chennai, we're bringing the recruitment community together through meetups, mentorship, podcasts, and masterclasses.
